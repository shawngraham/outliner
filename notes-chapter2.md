# Chapter 2: Literature Review
A comprehensive review synthesizes existing research to identify gaps and establish theoretical frameworks.
#-------
# Purpose of Literature Review
- Establish context for your research
- Identify gaps in existing knowledge
- Build on previous work
- Demonstrate scholarly understanding
#-------
# Search Strategies
Use academic databases (JSTOR, Google Scholar, PubMed) with relevant keywords. Track your sources systematically.
#-------
# Critical Analysis
Don't just summarize - analyze methodologies, compare findings, identify contradictions, and evaluate theoretical frameworks.
#-------
# Synthesizing Sources
Group sources by themes or methodologies. Show how different perspectives relate to each other and your research question.
#-------
# Writing the Review
Organize chronologically, thematically, or methodologically. Maintain critical voice while being fair to source authors.
#-------
# Common Pitfalls
- Lack of organization
- Pure summary without analysis
- Insufficient coverage
- Over-reliance on secondary sources
